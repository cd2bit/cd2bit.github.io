Chicago Deaf/DeafBlind in Tech (CD2BiT) is a co-operative initiative of designers, developers, and STE(A)M professionals. Majority-operated by deaf, hard-of-hearing (hoh) and deafblind (db) professionals in the Chicago area.

We help organizations, education and events achieve greater impact through technology for the deaf/hoh/db.
