Empowering and mentoring deaf/hard-of-hearing/deafblind learners to succeed in STEM careers.

Chicago Deaf/DeafBlind in Tech (CD2BiT) is a co-operative initiative of designers, developers, and STEM professionals and students. Majority-operated by deaf (d), hard-of-hearing (hoh) and deafblind (db) in the Chicago area.

We help organizations, education and events achieve greater impact through technology for the d/hoh/db.
